Why Dynamic QR Codes?

Traditional QR codes embed the target link directly, making them break if the link dies. QRAurify points to a redirects manager you can change in 1 second.

Save Printing Costs

Keep your packaging, menus, and brochures intact. Change files or destination links instantly without ordering new prints.

Real-time Analytics

Track every scan. See which devices (Mobile vs. Desktop) and what platforms (Google, Social) refer users to your pages.

Permanent Redirect Resilience

If you build a new website, your old QR codes won't break. Just log in, replace the destination URL, and enjoy uninterrupted service.

Time-Based Smart Menus

Serve different menus or campaigns automatically at different times of the day (e.g. morning breakfast vs. evening dinner) with the same QR code.

Custom QR Styling & Logos

Customize colors to fit your brand identity. Upload your company logo to place in the center of the QR pattern for a premium look.

White-Label & Bulk Import

Generate codes in bulk by pasting CSV lists. Redirect through your own custom domains (e.g. qr.mybrand.com) for a 100% white-label feel.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I reach the 100 scans limit on Free plan?
If your printed codes exceed 100 scans, they will temporarily display a warning page notifying users that the scan quota has been reached. You can unlock unlimited redirects instantly by upgrading to Pro for 500 TL/month.
Can I customize the shape and logo in the QR code?
Yes! With the Pro plan, you can change the foreground color of the QR code block and upload your business logo to overlay right in the center of the QR pattern.
Can I use my own custom domains?
Yes. While our default short link starts with qraurify.co/r/, Pro subscribers can request custom domain redirects to match their exact brand address.
